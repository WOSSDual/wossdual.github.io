About the WOSS Dual Olympiad

The WOSS Dual Olympiad is a Math and Computer Science Competition, held in the WOSS South Campus Cafeteria, between December XX and December XX.

It is held on two seperate days, with two rounds within one day - the first day is for Math, where participants will be solving math problems either in the individual round, or the team round.

The second day will be focusing on Computer Science, also with an individual and a team round. Participants will be solving programming problems on DMOJ with their language of choice.

Participants may participate in any or all four rounds.
